Lekang Jiang

lj408@cam.ac.uk

I am a PhD student in the Department of Engineering at University of Cambridge, Churchill College, supervised by Dr.Stephan Goetz. Prior to this, I obtained my master degree in Advanced Computer Science at University of Cambridge and bachelor degree in Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence at the University of Nottingham.

My research direction is the application of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Large Language Models (LLMs), especially in the patent domain, including patent analysis and patent text generation. If you are interested in related topics, please feel free to contact me.
